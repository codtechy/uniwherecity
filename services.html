What We Do?

Career Counsiling

Our team of experienced counsellors is aware that selecting the correct course and college is an important choice that will have a big impact on a student's future. We evaluate each student's interests, strengths, and career objectives through individualized sessions. We offer thorough counsel to assist students in making decisions that are in line with their abilities and ambitions.

Admission Support

It might be intimidating when dealing with the overseas university admissions process. At UniWhereCity, we make this process easier by utilizing our broad network. We help students with every stage of the process, from completing application forms to obtaining necessary paperwork and following to deadlines. Our purpose is to make sure that students are granted admission into prestigious international government or private universities that best fit their academic backgrounds and personal preferences.

⁠Visa Processing

Obtaining a student visa is a crucial aspect of studying abroad. Our dedicated visa processing team provides end-to-end support to students, guiding them through the visa application process and assisting with documentation requirements. We ensure that all necessary paperwork is in order and help students prepare for visa interviews, making the transition to their chosen destination as smooth as possible.

Comfortable Living with Exclusive Hostel Facilities

Feeling homesick is natural. We offer exclusive hostel facilities specifically catering to Indian students. This provides a comfortable and familiar living experience while you adjust to your new surroundings. Our exclusive hostel facilities provide students with comfortable accommodation, while our Kerala-style mess ensures nutritious and homely meals.

Assistance with Admissions to Domestic Colleges

UniWhereCity is not only an expert in helping students gain admission to international universities, but we also use our knowledge to help students get into domestic colleges in a variety of fields, such as engineering, medicine, paramedical, and more. Our committed staff offers thorough support during the admissions process, making sure that students are guided and helped at every stage.

Do you dream of studying abroad but feel overwhelmed by the options?  Then we can be your guide!
